Story

'Transformers,' an alien creature with far superior intelligence and power than mankind. The Transformers, divided into the Autobots Corps that protects justice and the Decepticon Corps that represents evil, have long fought for the ultimate energy source, the Cube. But the Cube disappeared somewhere in space due to a planet explosion. They wander all over the universe in search of the 'Cube' to put an end to the long war. The "Cube," an energy source that will determine the future of mankind, wanders through space and falls on Earth, and finds out that the "Cube" is on Earth after persistent tracking by the Decepticon Corps. They choose the earth as the stage of the final war, and sneak into it without anyone knowing. Transformers, who can invade any planet, transform itself into machines existing there, and hide their existence, transform into various forms of cars, helicopters, and fighter jets, penetrating deeply into human life. Then they find out the existence of Sam, the main character who has the crucial key to finding the cube's location. Living with no idea of all this, "Sam" witnesses an amazing scene one night when his old car turns into a giant robot. His car is the "Bumblebee" of the "Transformers" of the "Autobot" corps that defends justice and is sent to protect Sam against the "Decepticon" corps that conspires to take the Cube. Bumblebee, who must protect Sam to protect the order of the universe, begins to bring his brothers, the Autobots, to Earth. It is necessary to prevent the huge energy source "Cube" from falling into the hands of Decepticons. There will be a breathtaking confrontation between the evil "Decepticon" Corps, which seeks to find Sam and hold the Cube in its hands, and the "Autobot" Corps of Justice, which seeks to protect the Earth, over the fate of mankind.
